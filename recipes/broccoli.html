Chicken w/ Broccoli

A very simple and easy-to-prepare recipe that is both healthy and budget-friendly.

Ingredients: (good for 4 people)
  • 1 lb Chicken breast sliced into thin pieces
  • 1 small head Broccoli chopped into florets
  • 1 medium yellow Onion sliced
  • 5 cloves Garlic minced
  • 1 pc Egg beaten
  • 3/4 cup Cornstarch
  • 2 tsp Cornstarch diluted in 1/2 cup Water
  • 2 1/2 tbsp Oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp Soy sauce
  • 3/4 cup Cooking oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
Instructions: (the way I like it)
  1. In a bowl, mix the sliced Chicken, Cornstarch, beaten Egg, Salt, and Pepper.

  2. In medium heat, pour the Cooking oil onto a pan. Dip the coated Chicken and fry until light brown, then set aside.

  3. Sauté the Garlic and Onion.

  4. Add Oyster sauce and the diluted Cornstarch.

  5. Put in the fried Chicken and cook for 3 minutes.

  6. Add the Broccoli florets. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes more.

  7. It's ready! Serve with steamed rice and Enjoy!
