Tuna Pesto w/ Penne Pasta

A twist to an Italian staple, this dish is perfect for working adults who only got so much time to prepare good meals.

Ingredients: (good for 4 people)
  • 500g Penne pasta
  • 2 tbsp Pesto sauce Note: I use ready-made Pesto sauce.
  • 3 cloves Garlic minced
  • 1 half Onion minced
  • 1 slice Cheddar cheese
  • 1 can of Tuna flakes in oil
  • 1 tsp Basil minced
  • 1 tsp Parsley minced
  • 3 tbsp Olive oil
Instructions: (the way I like it)
  1. Cook Penne pasta in boiling water for 8-10 mins.
  2. In medium heat, pour Olive oil onto a pan.
  3. Sauté the Garlic and Onion until Garlic turns light brown.
  4. Add the Tuna, Pesto sauce, and Cheddar cheese.
  5. Add the Penne pasta and mix the ingredients.
  6. Add the Basil and Parsley for added aroma.
  7. It's Ready! Serve with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and Enjoy!
