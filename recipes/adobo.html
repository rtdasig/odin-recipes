Chicken Adobo

Adobo is a style of cooking that involves marinating the main ingredient into a mix of vinegar and soy sauce.
Native in the Philippines, this chicken dish is a comfort food for all Filipinos wherever we are in the world.

Ingredients: (good for 4 people)
  • 2 lbs Chicken
  • 8 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 4 tbsp White vinegar
  • 5 cloves Garlic
  • 1 tsp Whole peppercorn
  • 3 pcs Dried bay leaves
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1 1/2 cups Water
  • 3 tbsp Cooking oil
Instructions: (the way I like it)
  1. Add the Chicken into a bowl with Soy sauce, White vinegar, Garlic, Whole peppercorn, and Dried Bay leaves.
  2. Pour cooking oil onto the heated pan.
  3. Pan-fry the Chicken (remove from the sauce) for 2 minutes per side (or longer if you prefer), then set aside.
  4. On a pot, sauté the Garlic until the color turns to light brown. Note: this is a key step as my key ingredient is Garlic
  5. Pour the sauce onto the pan, then add Water.
  6. Add the Chicken. Simmer for 30 mins. or until the Chicken gets tender.
  7. Add Vinegar and/or Soy sauce and cook for 10 mins.
  8. To taste, put in the Sugar, Salt, Pepper (this part here I will give to you lol). Stir, then turn the heat off.
  9. It's Ready! Serve hot and Enjoy!
