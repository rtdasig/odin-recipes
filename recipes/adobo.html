Chicken Adobo

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 lbs Chicken
  • 8 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 4 tbsp White vinegar
  • 5 cloves Garlic
  • 1 tsp Whole peppercorn
  • 3 pcs Dried bay leaves
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1 1/2 cups Water
  • 3 tbsp Cooking oil
Four (4) people
45mins.

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Add Chicken into a bowl with Soy sauce, White vinegar, Garlic, Whole peppercorn, Dried Bay leaves.
  2. Pour cooking oil onto the heated pan.
  3. Pan-fry the Chicken (removed from the sauce) for 2 minutes per side (or longer), then set aside.
  4. On a pot, sauté the Garlic until the color turns to light brown.
    Note: this is a key step as my key ingredient is Garlic
  5. Pour the sauce onto the pan, then add Water.
  6. Add the Chicken. Simmer for 30 mins. or until the Chicken gets tender.
  7. Add Vinegar and/or Soy sauce and cook for 10 mins.
  8. To taste, put in the Sugar, Salt, Pepper. Stir, then turn the heat off.
  9. It's Ready! Serve hot and Enjoy!
